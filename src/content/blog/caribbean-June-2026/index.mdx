Caribbean night might have been our favourite one yet.

It was our biggest group at the table so far, and somehow every single dish landed. Jerk chicken, pepper shrimp, rice and peas, plantain in about four different forms — we lost count of how many times someone went back for seconds.

![Caribbean Night table spread](./event 1.jpeg)

The room was loud in the best way, and it felt like everyone had been waiting for an excuse to cook this cuisine.

![Dishes at Caribbean Night](./event 2.jpeg)

A big thank you to **Sunny Daze** for keeping us all cool with their lemonade all night. It disappeared fast.
